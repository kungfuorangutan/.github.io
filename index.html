My Values

Focus on You
My goal is to help you feel stronger and more powerful. I believe in affirming coaching, where you can show up as you are.
Consent
You control the pace and intensity. I will always ask for consent before cuing you, and you can revoke it at any time.
Accessibility
I strive to make my coaching accessible to as many people as possible, including pricing.
Progress
I will continue to learn how to support you. Your feedback is invaluable in helping me improve.
Inclusivity
I work with queer folks in a supportive environment that overcomes fitness industry norms and celebrates all the things your body can do.
Functional
We focus on preparing your body for your demands, prioritising strength, coordination & mobility for a healthy, active lifestyle.
Trauma-Informed
I take a trauma-informed approach, promoting well-being. I work with a charted psychologist to provide this.

Services

Personal Training
One-on-one sessions tailored to your needs and goals, whether in-person or online.
Group Classes
Inclusive group classes designed to foster a sense of community and mutual support.
Online Programs
Customizable online training programs that you can follow at your own pace.
Consultations
Initial consultations to assess your needs, set goals, and design a personalized fitness plan.
Specialized Training
Programs designed for specific populations, including those with disabilities, larger bodies, or those recovering from trauma.
Signposting
I also work with a qualified chartered psychologist and therapist who is available for signposting on to appropriate services, and advice.
